I have a background in statistical physics and computational biology. My research focuses on developing and applying AI methods to molecular biology and protein design, targeting medical applications. At the intersection of machine learning and experimental biology, I develop novel approaches using deep sequencing data to train AI models for generating optimized biomolecules. My work combines fundamental research with practical applications, including the design of specific antibodies, cancer-targeting peptides, and viral capsids, through collaborations with experimental groups.
